How It Works

Simple, transparent, and fully supported from start to finish.

01
📅

Book a Free Zoom Call

Schedule a 30-minute call with our placement team. We discuss your clinical goals, preferred destination, specialty, duration, and optional safari add-ons. Timezone automatically adjusted.

Schedule Zoom →
02
📋

Complete Your Application

Fill our 4-step online form — personal details, placement preferences (city, weeks, specialty), safari options, and a short motivation statement. Takes about 10 minutes. No payment required at this stage.

Start Application →
03
✅

Get Reviewed & Approved

Our team reviews your application within 24–48 hours. If approved, you'll receive a personalised placement offer with your destination, dates, and final pricing. A secure $200 deposit link is sent via email to confirm your place.

04
💳

Pay $200 Deposit via Stripe

Once approved, pay the secure $200 deposit via Stripe to lock in your placement. The deposit is deducted from your total package cost. Remaining balance is due 4 weeks before your start date.

05
📁

Upload Documents

After confirmation, upload your documents via your client dashboard: passport scan, medical certificate, travel insurance, and university letter. Your coordinator reviews and approves each one.

06
✈️

Arrive & Begin

Your coordinator meets you at the airport. You'll settle into your furnished accommodation, attend a pre-placement orientation, and begin your rotation the following day. 24/7 support is always available.

07
🏆

Complete & Receive Certificate

At the end of your placement, you receive a signed Certificate of Completion with your specialty, supervisor details, and hours logged — suitable for submission to your home institution for credit review.
