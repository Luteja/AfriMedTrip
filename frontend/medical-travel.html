Our Process

How Medical Travel Works

From your first enquiry in Dar es Salaam to returning home recovered — we handle everything.

01 📞

Free Consultation

Contact us with your medical records and diagnosis. Our medical coordinator assesses your case and recommends the best destination and hospital.

02 🏥

Hospital Appointment

We submit your medical records to partner hospitals, coordinate specialist review, confirm your appointment and obtain a treatment quote.

03 ✈️

Travel & Arrival

We arrange your medical visa, flights, and airport transfer. A local coordinator meets you and escorts you to the hospital or accommodation.

04 🏠

Treatment & Return

Receive your treatment with a dedicated case manager present. After recovery, we coordinate your safe return and arrange follow-up care in Tanzania.
